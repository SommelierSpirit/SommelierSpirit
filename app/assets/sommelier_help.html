The Sommelier Spirit


Welcome to the latest in menus.  The Sommelier Spirit allows you to select from the wide variety of drink selections offered by this restaurant.  You have the opportunity to select wines, spirits, beers and specialty drinks to fulfill your dining experience.

The Sommelier Spirit will also allow registered guests to recall your previous purchases, your personal ratings and selections you marked to review at a future date.  Don’t worry, if you don’t register you will still have access to the complete selection process.

For Registered guests, you may opt into restaurant and spirit suppliers email notifications of specials and new products or services.  We hope you enjoy using The Sommelier Spirit and would appreciate any feedback to further improve this menu.
